Safety Standards & CSAE Policy

Effective Date: December 24, 2025

This document outlines the safety standards for the Sri Bagavath mobile application. Our mission is to provide a safe, spiritual, and educational environment for all seekers of truth.

Zero Tolerance for CSAE

Sri Bagavath maintains a zero-tolerance policy toward Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) and any form of Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE).

Content Safety and Moderation

The Sri Bagavath app is primarily a consumption-based platform providing spiritual teachings, schedules, and books. We do not currently allow users to upload, post, or share public content (images, videos, or text) within the app.

Because there is no user-generated public content, the risk of CSAE material being shared is strictly mitigated by our platform's design.

Prohibited Activities

Any attempt to use our registration or internal systems to facilitate, promote, or share CSAE material will result in an immediate and permanent ban of the user account and, where required by law, a report to the appropriate law enforcement authorities including the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC).
