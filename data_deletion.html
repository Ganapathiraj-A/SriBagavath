Data Deletion Request

At Sri Bagavath, we respect your privacy and provide you with the option to delete your account and any associated personal data.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request the deletion of your account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

Send an email to: ganapathirajangappan82@gmail.com

Subject: Data Deletion Request - Sri Bagavath App

In your email, please include the email address associated with your Google Sign-In account so we can verify your identity and process the request.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Upon verification, we will permanently delete the following information from our Firebase database:

  • Your Name and Profile Picture URL
  • Your Email Address
  • Your unique User ID (UID)
  • Any program registration history associated with your account

Data Retention

Please note that some data may be retained for a limited period if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (such as financial records of donations, if applicable). However, all personal profile data will be purged from our active user databases within 30 days of your request.
